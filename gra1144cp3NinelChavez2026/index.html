Synopsis

The story follows Chihiro, a ten-year-old girl who stumbles into a mysterious spirit world after her parents are transformed into pigs. Trapped in this strange realm filled with gods, witches, and unusual creatures, she must work at a magical bathhouse run by the powerful witch Yubaba to survive and find a way to save her parents. Along the way, Chihiro forms an unforgettable bond with the mysterious Haku and learns courage, independence, and compassion. The film blends breathtaking fantasy with a heartfelt coming-of-age journey, making it one of the most beloved animated movies of all time.
